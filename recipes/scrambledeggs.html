Scrambled Eggs with Ham & Mozzarella Cheese Sandwhich

Description

This recipe will guide you on how to create an easy but delicious breakfast sandwhich to kickstart your day with a great meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Ham
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Wheat Bread
  • Butter
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut ham into small thin strips
  2. Shred Mozzarella cheese into small pieces
  3. Warm up a pan
  4. Cut a slice of butter and place into pan
  5. Crack 2 eggs and pour them into the pan
  6. Pour ham strips into the pan
  7. Pour shredded cheese into the pan
  8. Pour a small bit of black pepper into the entire pan
  9. Mix contents with a wooden spoon
  10. Keep mixing until everything is mixed and cooked well
  11. Turn off the stove heat
  12. Get 2 slices of wheat bread
  13. Using a spoon, scoop up scrambled eggs and place onto a bread slice
  14. Merge both bread slices
  15. Enjoy!
